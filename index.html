Civic Education Kenya - Empowering Citizens Through Education

Interactive Platform for Democratic Participation and Governance Learning

The Civic Education Kenya App (CEKA) is a comprehensive digital platform designed to empower Kenyan citizens through accessible, interactive civic education. Our mission is to strengthen democracy by ensuring every citizen understands their rights, responsibilities, and role in governance.

Platform Features

Discover interactive learning modules covering the Kenyan Constitution, governance structures, electoral processes, and citizen participation. Access educational resources, community forums, civic participation tools, and real-time information about democratic processes in Kenya.

Educational Content

Comprehensive curriculum covering constitutional law, human rights, government structure, electoral systems, public participation, accountability mechanisms, and civic duties. Content designed for learners of all levels, from students to adult citizens seeking to enhance their civic knowledge.

Community Engagement

Connect with fellow citizens, participate in discussions about governance issues, share experiences, and collaborate on civic initiatives. Our platform fosters informed dialogue and collective action for positive change in Kenya.

Recall254 & NASAKA IEBC Map

Access the NASAKA IEBC map to find IEBC polling centers and stay updated on Voter Registration Kenya 2026 and democratic participation.

Kenya Bill Tracker & Public Participation

Get real-time Parliament of Kenya updates, track the Finance Bill, and engage in Public Participation Kenya regarding Devolution and County Government functions.

Governance & Social Justice Kenya

Monitor Controller of Budget reports and advocate for youth leadership Kenya through our comprehensive political education and civic awareness tools.

Key Topics Covered

  • Kenyan Constitution and Bill of Rights
  • Structure of Government - Executive, Legislative, Judiciary
  • Electoral Processes and Voting Rights
  • Public Participation and Citizen Engagement
  • Accountability and Transparency in Governance
  • County Government and Devolution
  • Anti-Corruption and Ethics
  • Access to Information and Freedom of Expression
  • Gender Equality and Social Justice
  • Youth Participation in Democracy

Target Audience

Students, educators, civil society organizations, government officials, community leaders, youth groups, women's organizations, religious institutions, media practitioners, and all Kenyan citizens committed to strengthening democracy and good governance.
